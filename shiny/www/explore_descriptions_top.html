Explore terms that affect the success or failure of Kickstarter campaigns for tabletop games. Terms can be sorted by:

  • Coefficient: coefficients from the logistic regression with lasso regularization (see full report for details)
  • Frequency: the proportion of campaigns containing the term
  • # of models: the number of iterations of the model that retained the term with a non-zero coefficient (out of 100)

Terms with postive coefficients are in blue. Those with negative coefficients are in red.
